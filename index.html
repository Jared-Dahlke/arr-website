Common questions

Roofing questions, answered straight

How much does a roof inspection cost?

Nothing — our roof inspections are free, with no obligation. We take a thorough look at your roof's condition and give you a straight answer: repair it, replace it, or leave it alone.

Can you help if my insurance claim was denied?

Yes. We've handled over 100 insurance projects and have helped homeowners get previously denied claims approved for full roof replacement. We document the damage properly — photos, measurements, and a written assessment — and work with your insurance company throughout the process.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Gardendale, AL and serve the Birmingham metro area — including Fultondale, Mount Olive, Morris, Kimberly, Warrior, Trussville, and Birmingham itself. If you're nearby but not sure, call (205) 369-9630 and ask.

What kinds of roofs do you install?

Shingle roofs — wood, slate, metal, and composite shingles — and metal roofing in a range of coatings and colors supplied by Sloan Supply. We're an Atlas Pro Plus Gold contractor and install the full Atlas roof system, which qualifies your roof for the best warranty Atlas offers.

Do you offer financing?

Yes — we're partnered with Alabama Power Smart Financing. We handle the application process with you, whether it's for part of the project or all of it.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Our technicians are fully licensed, bonded, and insured, and we've served Birmingham-area homeowners since 1998. We're also a BBB-accredited business.
